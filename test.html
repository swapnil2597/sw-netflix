About us

SMT is a Professionally Managed TechnologyMarketing company in Central India involving high-tech machine tools imported from industrially developed nations. The company represents some world-renowned manufacturers from Europe, the USA, and Far East Countries for Metal Cutting, Metal Forming Machines, and Metrology Equipment. The management includes professionals with 35+ years' experience in the Sales & Services of Imported Machines.

The objective is to serve the Engineering Industry in India by continuously introducing the latest innovations in manufacturing technologies involving Metal Cutting, Forming & Metrology.
